Privacy Policy for the Action Platformer

At our multiplayer game, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our players. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect:

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as name, email address, and age when you create an account with us.
  • Game Data: We may collect game data such as levels completed, statistics, and communication history between players.
  • Device Information: We may collect device information such as IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use Your Information:

  • To Provide Our Services: We use your information to provide our multiplayer game services, including communication features, level sharing, and cloud data backups.
  • To Improve Our Services: We use your information to improve our multiplayer game services and develop new features.
  • To Communicate With You: We may use your email address to send you updates about our multiplayer game services, including new features and promotions.

How We Protect Your Information:

  • Security Measures: We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse.
  • Data Backup: We regularly backup your game data to the cloud to prevent data loss in case of a system failure or other unforeseen circumstances.
  • Third-Party Providers: We may use third-party providers to store and process your information. These providers are subject to strict confidentiality and security requirements.

Sharing Your Information:

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you give us your consent.
  • Legal Obligations: We may share your information if required by law or government authorities.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy:

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website and will take effect immediately upon posting.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at support@mrboomdev.ru.
